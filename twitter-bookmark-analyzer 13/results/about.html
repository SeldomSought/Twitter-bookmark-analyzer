Consciousness Analyzer

Version 0.1.0

What is this?

The Consciousness Analyzer transforms your Twitter/X bookmarks into deep psychological insights. It reveals the hidden patterns in what you save—your core interests, cognitive style, trusted voices, and potential blind spots.

Think of it as a mirror for your digital mind.

Privacy First

Your data never leaves your device. Everything happens locally:

  • All analysis runs in your browser
  • Data stored in local IndexedDB
  • No accounts, no servers, no tracking
  • You can export or delete anytime

How It Works

When you click "Analyze", the extension opens your Twitter bookmarks in a background tab and captures the content. It then runs inference to identify topics, patterns, and psychological themes—all without sending data anywhere.

What You'll Discover

  • Your Archetype — Builder, Philosopher, Analyst, Creator, Strategist, or Seeker
  • Core Identity — Top themes that define your interests
  • Cognitive Style — How you process information
  • Trusted Voices — Authors you return to most
  • Temporal Patterns — When you're most curious
  • Blind Spots — What might be missing from your diet
